Parkhound

Parkhound is a marketplace that connects drivers looking for parking with local property owners who have spare parking spaces.

I work closely with co-founders Michael and Rob to help revolutionise the parking industry in Australia. We initially created a friendly brand , which instills trust which is always important when dealing with payments.

I designed an engaging landing page which serves dual purposes to attract both buyers and sellers in order to help the marketplace succeed.

I designed a fully functioning web app which allows users to search for spaces and manage their listings and bookings. The app is designed mobile first and is fully responsive ensuring that users have a seemless experience no matter what device they are accessing it from.

I also designed an iPhone app which takes advantage of native features such as location services and push notifications to enhance the experience for our users.
